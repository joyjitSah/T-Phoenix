Company

Located in Dhaka, Bangladesh, T-Phoenix is an offshore organization providing digital services to brands & companies including Graphic Design, Web & Image Editing. We specialize in delivering flexible solutions to clients both offline and online production. Our centralized setup & experienced team ensures effective collaboration to meet the needs of constantly changing markets and consumer behavior. T-Phoenix’s 24-hour efficient operation makes it possible to offer low cost, high quality productions that reduce traditional time-to-market windows, irrespective of location, time zones and the clients’ operating hours.

People

We believe in teamwork, doing and getting things done, collaboration, showing passion through our work to make differences to other lives’ is what makes T-Phoenix tick 24 hours a day, every day. We blend experience, fresh ideas, thriving & positive attitude, creativity to our every single work, doing so we bring young, skilled & dedicated individuals who constantly challenge themselves to come up with new, interesting things & have them trained, mentored & guided by our experienced personnels who have more than 7 years of experience. We welcome diversity and provide encouragement and career development opportunities to all levels of staff. T-Phoenix is committed to attracting, motivating and retaining the best talent, and will maintain our reputation as a company of choice with the most competitive employee benefits. We prioritise our culture first, skills and experience second. We listen to our clients as they are the one who help us to improve every single day.
